Privacy Policy for Mallangii

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Mallangii is a simple digital sensory toy app. This app does not collect, store, share, sell, or track any personal data.

Data Collection

Mallangii does not collect any user data.

Tracking

Mallangii does not track users across apps or websites.

Accounts

Mallangii does not require account creation or login.

Analytics and Advertising

Mallangii does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Network

Mallangii does not provide network features and does not send user activity to external servers.

Device Features

Mallangii uses haptic feedback on supported devices and plays sound effects bundled with the app. The app does not request access to the camera, microphone, photos, contacts, or location.

Children's Privacy

Mallangii does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at mallang.support@gmail.com.
