Mallangii Support

A soft digital sensory toy for short breaks.

How to use Mallangi

  • Touch or press the squishy on the screen.
  • Use multiple fingers to see soft multi-touch deformation.
  • Use two fingers to stretch the squishy.
  • Change the jelly color or squishy type from the controls.

Troubleshooting

  • If haptic feedback is not noticeable, check your device haptics settings.
  • If sound is not audible, check your device volume and silent mode.
  • If motion feels uncomfortable, enable Reduce Motion in iOS Accessibility settings.
